Our Campuses

The main campus of Walchand College of Engineering is located in Sangli. It is a spread over an area of 90 acres of land.

Our Facilities

A wide of range of facilities have been provided to the students to ensure a conducive learning atmosphere as well as a pleasant stay in campus.
WCE ensures that various aspects of student welfare have been well taken care of.

Central Library

Central Library of WCE is functioning from 1947 . Library Building is centrally situated.

Sports

Walchand College of Engineering Sangli has a range of facilities including a football ground, cricket ground, basketball court, lawn tennis court, volleyball court, and indoor gymnasium. There is also a running/jogging track on the campus.

Food & Canteens

In addition to the regular mess facilities in the hostels, the Walchand College of Engineering Sangli campus boasts of affordable canteens spread throughout the campus. All canteens provide delicious food at reasonable prices.
